Folia


Game Design Document

Summary


Explore a stylized forest in VR searching for clues as to why you're here. Inspired by games like Firewatch, Breath of the Wild, Sea Of Thieves and The Forest, we want this game to be more of a visual/audio experience rather than a mechanic based traditional game, slightly like Abzu, the underwater exploration game.
